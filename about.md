## About Me

I am an Associate Professor at Design and Analysis of Communication Systems (DACS) and affiliated with EDGE research center at the University of Twente. 

On March 2017, I am granted the title of Docent in Computer Science at the University of Helsinki, Finland. 

I act also as the Scientific Director of <a href="https://www.4tu.nl/nirict/" target="_blank">4TU.NIRICT</a> as of January 2025. 

My research aims at understanding, designing, and developing solutions for efficient networking, mostly in the context resource allocation in wireless networks considering energy-efficiency and resilience. 

You can see my UT profile page <a href="https://people.utwente.nl/s.bayhan" target="_blank">here</a>. 

Also, here is a <a href="https://www.utwente.nl/en/news/2019/9/7554/welcoming-suzan-bayhan-as-a-new-employee-in-the-computer-science-department?code=450758fa"  target="_blank">short introduction</a> of myself (2019) and a short interview at I/O Magazine (July 2022).

During 2017-2018, I was on N2Women Board as one of the mentoring co-chairs. I had my PhD degree from Bogazici University, Turkey in 2012.
